About

Me

My passion for coding began during a Cyber Defense Certificate Program that I completed with the University of Central Florida in 2021. We studied a wide variety of topics that included Network Security, Ethical Hacking, and Game Theory. However, there was one topic that we covered where I found myself constantly studying and working outside of class. Python. I wasn't pouring myself into this subject because I felt as if I had some vast misunderstanding of it. No. I was simply having too much fun.

Therefore, I went back to my University and enrolled in their Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp.

However, my life isn't all screens and technology! I also enjoy playing video games (I know...), writing and recording music, and laying back while sipping on a coke and watching the latest and greatest TV show. (Ok, I was wrong. I love technology)!

I am happily married to my gorgeous wife, Willa-Ann. We have two precious little boys that we love with all our heart. When I'm not typing away at my computer or playing music you'd catch me snuggling with one of them.

I am excited to share this passion of mine and am looking forward to hearing from you!
